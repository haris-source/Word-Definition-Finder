Word Definition Finder

About Project

The Word Definition Finder is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help users quickly discover the meanings of English words. Whether you're a student, writer, or language enthusiast, this application makes it easy to look up definitions in real-time using a clean and intuitive interface. Powered by modern web technologies and connected to a dictionary API, the tool fetches accurate definitions instantly. Just type in a word and click “Search Now” — the definition appears within seconds. It's lightweight, responsive, and perfect for enhancing your vocabulary on the go.
